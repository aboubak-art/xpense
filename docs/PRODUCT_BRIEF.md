# Xpense - Product Brief

## Vision
Xpense is the most delightful expense tracker ever built. It combines powerful analytics with an interface so intuitive it feels like second nature. Every interaction is crafted to make financial awareness effortless — from lightning-fast expense entry to insights that actually change behavior.

## Target Audience
- Primary: Young professionals (22-35) who want control over their spending without the complexity of traditional finance apps
- Secondary: Budget-conscious families and students tracking shared or individual expenses
- Tertiary: Freelancers and small business owners tracking business expenses separately

## Key Differentiators
1. **Zero-friction entry**: Add an expense in under 3 seconds with smart defaults and intelligent categorization
2. **Meaningful stats, not just charts**: AI-powered insights that tell you "You spend 40% more on dining out during stressful weeks" not just "Food: $450"
3. **True offline-first**: Works completely without internet; sync is invisible and conflict-free
4. **Haptic-rich interactions**: Every meaningful action provides tactile confirmation — success pulses, warning rumbles, satisfying clicks
5. **Privacy-first**: Your data is yours. End-to-end encrypted cloud backup with local-only mode available

## Success Metrics
- Day-1 retention: >60%
- Day-7 retention: >35%
- Average expense entry time: <3 seconds
- Monthly active users adding 10+ expenses/week: >40%
- App Store rating: >4.7 stars

## Monetization
- Free tier: Full functionality, local storage only
- Premium ($3.99/month or $29.99/year): Cloud sync, unlimited backup history, advanced analytics, custom categories, multi-currency support, receipt scanning

## Platforms
- iOS 15+ (primary)
- Android 10+ (secondary, share 90% codebase via Flutter)
- Potential web dashboard (future)
